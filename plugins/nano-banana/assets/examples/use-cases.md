## Tips for All Use Cases

1. **Specify the platform** - Different platforms have different optimal dimensions
2. **Leave space for text** - Many marketing uses need room for overlays
3. **Consider the mood** - Match the atmosphere to the brand/purpose
4. **Request specific styles** - Photo, illustration, 3D, etc.
5. **Include quality modifiers** - 4K, professional, commercial quality
6. **Describe lighting** - Critical for setting the right tone
