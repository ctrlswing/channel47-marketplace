## Style Characteristics

- Forward-leaning italic appearance
- Clean, professional look
- Good for: Modern tech logos, dynamic headers
- Height: 5 lines per character
- Works well at various sizes

## Usage Notes

- Slant creates a sense of motion and modernity
- Best for short words or phrases
- Maintains readability while adding visual interest
- Works well combined with other text styles
