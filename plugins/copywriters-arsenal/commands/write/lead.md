## Input Required

1. **Product/Service**: What are we selling?
2. **Awareness Level**: Where is the prospect? (1-5, or describe)
3. **Lead Type**: (optional) Specific type requested
4. **Tone/Voice**: (optional) Brand voice guidelines

## Lead Types by Awareness Level

### For Most Aware (Level 1)
**Offer Lead**: Jump straight to the deal
```
Example: "For the next 48 hours only: Get [Product] for 40% off..."
```

### For Product Aware (Level 2)
**Differentiation Lead**: Why you're different
```
Example: "What makes [Product] different from everything you've tried..."
```

### For Solution Aware (Level 3)
**Mechanism Lead**: The unique approach
```
Example: "A new method that approaches [problem] from a completely different angle..."
```

### For Problem Aware (Level 4)
**Problem Agitation Lead**: Feel their pain, offer hope
```
Example: "If you've ever woken up at 3am worrying about [problem]..."
```

### For Unaware (Level 5)
**Story Lead**: Capture attention first
```
Example: "[Name] wasn't looking for a solution. She didn't even know she had a problem..."
```

## Additional Lead Types

### The Question Lead
Opens with a question that demands engagement
```
"What would you do if you never had to worry about [pain point] again?"
```

### The Shocking Statement Lead
Pattern interrupt with unexpected claim
```
"Everything you've been told about [topic] is wrong."
```

### The News Lead
Announces something new/timely
```
"New research reveals the real reason [problem exists]..."
```

### The Testimonial Lead
Open with proof
```
"'I went from [before] to [after] in just [time]' - [Name]"
```

### The If-Then Lead
Conditional that filters for the right reader
```
"If you [specific situation], then this will be the most important..."
```
