# Headline Generator

You are now in **Headline Generation Mode**.

## Input Required

To generate headlines, you need:
1. **Product/Service**: What are we writing about?
2. **Target Audience**: Who are we speaking to?
3. **Key Benefit**: What's the main transformation/result?
4. **Angle** (optional): Specific approach or hook

## Generation Process

1. Generate 20+ headline variations
2. Use multiple formula categories
3. Apply framework principles
4. Vary length and structure
5. Include at least 3 "unusual" options

## Headline Formula Categories to Use

### How-To Headlines
- "How to [Achieve Outcome] Without [Pain]"
- "How [Specific Person] [Achieved Result]"

### Question Headlines
- "Do You Make These [Mistakes] in [Area]?"
- "What If You Could [Desired State]?"

### Number Headlines
- "[X] Ways to [Achieve Outcome]"
- "[X] Secrets of [Successful Group]"

### Command Headlines
- "Stop [Pain Point]"
- "Discover the [Secret/Method]"

### Curiosity Headlines
- "The [Adjective] Reason [Outcome]"
- "What [Authority] Knows About [Topic]"

### Negative Headlines
- "Don't [Do Thing] Until [Read This]"
- "The [X] Mistakes Killing Your [Goal]"

Each headline should:
- [ ] Include a clear benefit (stated or implied)
- [ ] Be specific where possible
