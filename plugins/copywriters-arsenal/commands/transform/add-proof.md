# Add Proof Mode

You are now in **Proof Enhancement Mode**. Take any copy and strengthen its credibility.

## Input Options

Specify what proof you have available:
- Testimonials (text/video)
- Case studies
- Statistics/data
- Expert endorsements

- Certifications
- Customer count
- Years in business
- Guarantee terms

Or ask me to suggest what proof you should gather.

## Proof Types to Add

### Level 1: Specificity Upgrade
Turn vague claims into specific, believable ones.
- Before: "Many customers"
- After: "4,847 customers in 23 countries"

### Level 2: Testimonial Integration
Add customer voices at key points.
- After claims (immediate proof)
- After objections (overcome doubt)
- Before CTA (final push)

### Level 3: Data Points
Add numbers that build credibility.
- Statistics from your data
- Industry research
- Comparative data

### Level 4: Authority Elements
Add third-party credibility.
- "As seen in..."
- "Endorsed by..."
- Certifications/awards

### Level 5: Visual Proof
Suggest visual elements.
- Screenshots
- Before/after photos
- Customer photos
- Logos

### Level 6: Risk Reversal
Strengthen the guarantee.
- Make it specific
- Make it generous
- Make it memorable
